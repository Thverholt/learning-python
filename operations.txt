+ plus - this does addition
- minus - this does subtraction 
/ slash - this does division
* asterisk - this does multiplication
% percent - this is modulus, it gives remainder
< less-than - this shows when left side is smaller than right side
> greater-than - this shows when left side is greater than right side
<= less-than-equal - this shows when left side is smaller or equal than right side
>= greater-than-equal - this shows when right side is smaller or equal than left side
